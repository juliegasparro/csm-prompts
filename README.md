# CSM Prompt Library

Welcome! This library is a collection of ready-to-use AI prompt templates designed specifically for Customer Success Managers.

Each prompt helps you create professional, consistent communication in less time — without starting from a blank page. Whether you are writing a follow-up email after a customer call, preparing for a QBR, or handing off an account, these templates give you a reliable starting point you can customize to fit your voice and your customer.

🔗 [View the live library](https://juliegasparro.github.io/csm-prompts/)

## What Is This Library?

The CSM Prompt Library is a practical toolkit built for CSMs who want to use AI more effectively in their day-to-day work. Instead of writing prompts from scratch, you can use these templates to guide the AI toward outputs that are professional, on-brand, and relevant to your specific customer situation.

## Templates Included

1. Follow-Up Email
2. Style Block
3. QBR Agenda
4. Handoff Notes
5. External Research
6. O2 Framework Mapping
7. Share the Framework

## Who Is This For?

This library is for Customer Success Managers at any experience level who want to save time, improve consistency, and produce higher-quality customer-facing communication with the help of AI tools like Claude or ChatGPT.
